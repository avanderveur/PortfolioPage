Projects

IBM Data Science Capstone

This capstone project involves predicting the successful landing of the Falcon 9 first stage in SpaceX rocket launches. The goal is to determine whether the first stage will land, as this directly impacts the cost of the launch. SpaceX's ability to reuse the first stage significantly reduces launch costs compared to other providers. The predictive model's insights can be crucial for potential competitors bidding against SpaceX for rocket launches. Throughout the project, you will experience the end-to-end Data Science methodology, including tasks such as data collection, data wrangling, exploratory data analysis, data visualization, model development, evaluation, and reporting results to stakeholders. The project immerses you in the role of a Data Scientist working for a startup aiming to compete with SpaceX.
